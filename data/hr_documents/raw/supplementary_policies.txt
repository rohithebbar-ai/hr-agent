=== Probationary Period Policy ===
Section: Employment Policies
Category: Employment

All new employees at VanaciPrime serve a 90-day probationary period 
beginning on their first day of employment. This period allows both 
the employee and VanaciPrime to evaluate the fit of the role.

During the probationary period:
- Employees receive standard pay and basic benefits
- Performance is reviewed at 30, 60, and 90 days
- Either VanaciPrime or the employee may end the employment relationship 
  at any time, with or without cause, in accordance with the at-will 
  employment policy
- Health insurance enrollment becomes available on day 31 of employment
- PTO accrual begins immediately but cannot be used until completion 
  of the probationary period

Upon successful completion of the 90-day probationary period, employees 
transition to regular status with full eligibility for all VanaciPrime 
benefits programs. The Human Resources department will notify the 
employee and their supervisor when probationary status ends.

Questions about the probationary period should be directed to Human 
Resources at hr@vanaciprime.com.
